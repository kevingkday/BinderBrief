Why AI changes the rules of corporate liability

The article by Kyle Jeziorski highlights the growing challenge in commercial insurance as AI-generated liabilities rapidly increase, causing insurers to insert broad exclusions for AI risks into standard policies. This situation arises because traditional insurance policies do not adequately cover the probabilistic and multifaceted nature of AI risks, including financial loss, intellectual property issues, data leaks, physical harm, and property damage. The impact is a widening coverage gap that reduces broker margins and complicates underwriting, requiring new insurance products and active risk auditing by insureds.
